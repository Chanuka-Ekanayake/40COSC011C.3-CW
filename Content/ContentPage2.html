CHAPTER 2 - The Lungs of Sustainable Cities

Benefits of Urban Green Spaces

  1. Improved Air Quality: Green spaces act as natural air filters, absorbing pollutants and releasing oxygen.
  2. Climate Regulation: Trees and plants help reduce the urban heat island effect, lowering temperatures in cities.
  3. Biodiversity Support: Urban green areas provide habitats for various plant and animal species.
  4. Mental Health Benefits: Access to nature in cities reduces stress and improves overall mental wellbeing.
  5. Physical Health Promotion: Green spaces encourage outdoor activities and exercise
  6. Social Interaction: Parks and community gardens foster community engagement and social cohesion.

Challenges in Creating and Maintaining
Urban Green Spaces

  • Limited Space such as High-density urban areas often lack available land
    for new green spaces, Funding which Securing adequate funding for creation
    and maintenance can be difficult.Additionally, Competing Priorities which
    Urban development often prioritizes housing and commercial spaces over green
    areas and maintenance, which Ongoing care and upkeep of green spaces require
    consistent resources and effort.

Innovative Solutions

  1. Vertical Gardens: Utilization of building walls for growing plants.
  2. Green Roofs: Installation of vegetation on rooftops to create additional green space.
  3. Pocket Parks: Transformation of small, underused areas into green spaces.
  4. Urban Forests: Planting trees along streets and in available spaces to create urban canopies.
